This wedding is at Broadway Hall

Both our ceremony and reception will be at Broadway Hall, within a few blocks of downtown Bellingham. The brief ceremony will start at 6:30, followed by dinner and dancing until eleven or midnight. Do get a drink (in the upstairs reception bar area) before the ceremony starts, and feel free to keep sipping during the ceremony. We would, if we didn’t have to remember our vows.

This wedding is in Bellingham, City of Subdued Excitement

Bellingham is a university town where entertainment is easy to find. We recommend using one of the city’s many bike and walking trails to wander around Bellingham Bay or Whatcom Falls Park while enjoying the start of autumn. Our Farmers Market is a popular local hangout every Saturday, and a nice starting point for spending the day browsing downtown. Fairhaven (about 2.5 miles from downtown, and an easy walk on the South Bay Trail) also has a cute shopping district and a great local bookstore.

We highly recommend a drink at several of our local breweries. Go to Kulshan for a beer (and a rotating selection of food trucks). Chuckanut Brewery and Boundary Bay Brewery are both popular, with broad brewpub menus. Diamond Jim’s and the Little Cheerful are good breakfast/lunch spots. And if you want to hang out with (and drink like) the college kids, go to the Up and Up Tavern or the Ranch Room.
